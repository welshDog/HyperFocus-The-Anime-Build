# Make It Work — Shop Profile

**Owner:** Katrina "Kat"  
**Location:** The Sprawl, Nexus City  
**Type:** Independent workshop, repair shop, craft studio, and community hub  
**Tagline:** If you need it made, fixed, or figured out.

---

## The Philosophy

Kat never says no to a job.

If a customer asks for something she has never done before, she says yes, takes the cash, and works it out as she goes. She has pulled off jobs she had no business attempting. She has also nearly destroyed the workshop twice. Both times she finished the job.

---

## The Shop

- Sits on a quiet street in The Sprawl
- Hand-painted signs outside
- Warm glowing windows visible from the street
- Tools, art pieces, and experiments visible through the glass
- Feels alive and slightly chaotic from the outside
- Cash only. Paper records. No Grid connection. Untouchable by hackers.
- Storage space upstairs — currently occupied by Lydrix

---

## Inside

- Paint everywhere
- Half-finished 3D prints on shelves
- Glass pieces catching the light
- Tools covering every wall
- Sketches pinned up across every surface
- New experiments at every stage of completion
- A chair in the corner where Kat falls asleep when she stops moving
- Bolt's charging corner near the door, decorated with a small lightning bolt sticker

---

## What Kat Does

Every craft. All of them. Even ones she has never tried.

**Regular skills:**
- Painting
- 3D printing
- Glass blowing
- Sculpting
- Repair work
- Prototype building

**Jobs she said yes to before knowing how:**
- Stained glass window repair for a local pub
- Custom 3D printed prosthetic finger for an old man down the road
- Hand-blown glass trophy for a local football team
- Welded garden sculpture from scrap metal
- Leather-bound handmade book for a wedding
- Neon sign built from salvaged parts
- Ceramic mosaic mural for a community centre wall
- A working mechanical clock built from scratch
- Custom hand-painted skateboard deck
- A stuffed toy repair that turned into a full rebuild

She finished every single one.

---

## Lydrix's Role in the Shop

- Lives in the storage space upstairs
- Organises and tidies the workshop when the chaos gets too loud for his brain
- Kat hates this even though it helps
- Uses his photographic memory to find everything Kat loses
- Quietly helps with the technical side of jobs she has never done before
- Never officially works there. Definitely works there.

---

## Bolt's Role in the Shop

- Handles all deliveries and collections
- Carries messages when the Grid is unreliable
- Keeps a log of every customer interaction
- Puts a blanket on Kat when she falls asleep
- Maintains a personal list of rude customers
- Bolt's charging corner is the tidiest spot in the building

---

## Why the Shop Matters

Make It Work is the one place in Nexus City that the Grid cannot touch.

- No digital systems
- No hackable infrastructure
- No trail for The Static to follow
- Just cash, paper, craft, and people

In a city held together by technology, Kat's shop is the one thing that holds together without it. That makes it the emotional and practical heart of the whole story.
