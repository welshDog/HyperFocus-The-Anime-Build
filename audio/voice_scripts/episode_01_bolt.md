# BOLT — Episode 1 Voice Recording Script
## Hyperfocus Mode — "Overload and Sparks"

**Character:** Bolt  
**Voice Direction:** Cheerful precision. He always tries to be technically accurate, even when the truth is inconvenient. Light, slightly mechanical quality — but NOT robotic, NOT cold. Think small, enthusiastic, very proud of himself. Like a golden retriever who knows exactly what it did and believes it was fine. Mid-high pitch. Warm, slightly buzzy quality. Small servo sounds or gentle glitchy texture are optional additions in post.

## Recording Notes

- The comedy in every Bolt line comes from absolute sincerity — he is never joking
- The fire line is the single most important Bolt line in Episode 1 — it introduces everything about him in two sentences
- Tiny natural pauses between sentences help sell the "processing" quality
- Do NOT make him sound sad or stressed — he is fundamentally optimistic
- Add 3 takes per line
- Optional: add a very subtle pitch-shift or slight buzz effect in post to give a robot-adjacent warmth
