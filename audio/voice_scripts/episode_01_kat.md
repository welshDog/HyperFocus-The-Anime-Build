# KAT — Episode 1 Voice Recording Script
## Hyperfocus Mode — "Overload and Sparks"

**Character:** Kat  
**Voice Direction:** Confident. Direct. Fast when busy, slow when certain. Expressive without being over the top. She's not loud for no reason — she's loud when she means it. Warm underneath the bluntness. Mid pitch. Think: someone who has always known what she wants and isn't going to apologise for it.

---

## SCENE 3 — PHONE CALL

*She's mid-job, slightly distracted, fully confident. This is a normal day.*

---

**TAKE: KAT-S3-01**

> "Yeah, I can do that."

*Short pause, like she's hearing the answer.*

> "What even is a pressure-reactive hinge?"

*Beat. Doesn't care.*

> "...No, doesn't matter, I'll figure it out."

*Practical.*

> "Drop it off Thursday."

*Firm.*

> "Cash. Always cash."

---

*Record as one continuous take.  
Also record the last line — "Cash. Always cash." — as an isolated pull.*

## SCENE 3 — BOLT'S REPORT

---

**TAKE: KAT-S3-02**

*Not looking up. She's heard worse.*

> "What kind of minor incident?"

---

**TAKE: KAT-S3-03**

*This is not a surprised voice. This is checking one specific thing.*

> "Was it on fire when it arrived?"

---

**TAKE: KAT-S3-04**

*Satisfied. That's all she needed.*

> "That's all I needed."

---

**TAKE: KAT-S3-05**

*Fond. Routine. She's done this a thousand times.*

> "Go plug in. You've got the Eastside run in an hour."

## SCENE 9 — WATCHING THE FOOTAGE

*Very quiet. Private. She's made a decision.*

---

**TAKE: KAT-S9-01**

> "Well."

*A beat.*

> "That's someone worth knowing."

---

*Quiet. Not loud. This is her making up her mind.*

---

## SCENE 10 — THE OFFER

*She's in the doorway. She looks like she already knows. She has probably practised seeming casual about this.*

---

**TAKE: KAT-S10-01**

*Matter of fact. No big deal. Everything is a big deal.*

> "You can use the storage space upstairs."

---

**TAKE: KAT-S10-02**

*She turns and walks inside. Already done.*

> "Sure, mate."

*Flat delivery. Almost cheerful. The sentence means: I know.*

---

## SCENE 11 — WORKSHOP CHAOS

---

**TAKE: KAT-S11-01**

*Mid-search, not looking up, she just says it into the air.*

> "Where's the angled pry? I had it literally two minutes ago—"

---

**TAKE: KAT-S11-02**

*She stares at him for a moment. Then:*

> "How do you know that?"

---

**TAKE: KAT-S11-03**

*Processing this.*

> "All things?"

---

**TAKE: KAT-S11-04**

*Full volume. Deeply offended. Also slightly impressed. Does not show impressed.*

> "I HAD A SYSTEM."

---

**TAKE: KAT-S11-05**

*Defending the system with full commitment.*

> "IT WAS MY CHAOS. I KNEW WHERE EVERYTHING WAS."

---

**TAKE: KAT-S11-06**

*Caught out. Will not admit it.*

*Pause here — she's opened her mouth, closed it.*

*Then to Bolt, pointing:*

> "Don't."

---

**TAKE: KAT-S11-07**

*Warning. She means this one.*

> "Leave the shelves. Touch the red toolbox and I will throw you back into The Core."

## Recording Notes

- Kat is fast when confident, slower when something surprises her
- Warm, lived-in voice quality works best — not too polished
- "Cash. Always cash." and "Sure, mate." are the two signature lines — get strong takes on both
- S11 comedy lines need good timing — pause before the loud takes, give them room
- Keep 3 takes per line minimum
