# STATIC KING — Episode 1 Voice Recording Script
## Hyperfocus Mode — "Overload and Sparks"

**Character:** Static King  
**Voice Direction:** Deep. Unhurried. Every word costs something — so he doesn't waste them. He never raises his voice. He never needs to. The most dangerous energy in any room is stillness, and that's him. Think: someone who is twenty moves ahead and doesn't need to tell you. Warm low register. Pauses are part of the performance.

---

## SCENE 4 — THE TEST

---

**TAKE: SK-S4-01**

*He's still not looking at anyone. He says this as a correction, not a speech.*

> "This isn't breaking things."

*A beat. Let it land.*

> "This is a test."

---

**TAKE: SK-S4-02**

*Methodical. Certain.*

> "The Grid is strong. The people trust it."

*Pause.*

> "Before we tear it down — we need to know how fast it fights back."

*Now he looks at her.*

> "Do it. And watch what happens after."

## SCENE 8 — WATCHING LYDRIX

---

**TAKE: SK-S8-01**

*He holds up a hand to silence Overclock. One hand. Doesn't look. The room goes quiet.*

*Then:*

> "He built that in fifteen seconds."

*Beat.*

> "From scrap."

*Beat.*

> "While in a crowd."

*Pause. He moves toward the screen.*

> "And then he just... walked away."

*Even slower.*

> "No celebration. No fear. Like it was nothing."

---

**TAKE: SK-S8-02**

*Measured. He's already decided.*

> "Find out who he is. Where he goes. Who knows him."

*Beat.*

> "He's not an obstacle. Not yet."

*Pause.*

> "But if we leave him alone — he becomes one."

---

## SCENE 12 — VILLAIN TAG

---

**TAKE: SK-S12-01**

*He reads the dossier. Quiet recognition.*

> "He's been invisible on purpose."

*A beat.*

> "Smart."

---

**TAKE: SK-S12-02**

*Strategic. Each sentence placed like a chess piece.*

> "The cascade today was a test."

*Beat.*

> "The Grid recovered in fifteen seconds because of one person."

*He looks at the city.*

> "All future operations will account for him."

---

**TAKE: SK-S12-03**

*Very quiet. Direct.*

> "Find the place he stopped walking."

*Beat.*

> "Find the warm light."

---

**TAKE: SK-S12-04** *(final line — almost to himself — this is the most human he gets)*

*He's alone. He can afford one honest moment.*

> "You can't rebuild the world by fixing one junction at a time, kid."

*Long pause.*

> "But I understand the instinct."

## Recording Notes

- Pauses are instructions, not filler — follow them exactly
- The danger in Static King is quietness, not volume
- His final line in Scene 12 is the most important line in Episode 1 — do 5+ takes
- "Find the warm light." is the trailer hook line — get it perfect
- Deep, warm, unhurried mic placement — closer to the mic than usual
- No rushing. If a take feels too fast, it probably is
