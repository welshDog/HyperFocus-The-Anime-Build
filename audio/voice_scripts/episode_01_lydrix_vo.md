# LYDRIX — Episode 1 Voice Recording Script
## Hyperfocus Mode — "Overload and Sparks"

**Character:** Lydrix  
**Voice Direction:** Calm. Controlled. Dry. Never dramatic. He states things as fact, not feeling. Quiet confidence. Low-medium pitch. Think: someone who has been managing their own brain all their life and is very tired but very smart. NOT robotic — he's warm underneath, just guarded.

---

## PART 1 — VOICE OVER (Scene 2)

*Read slowly. Measured pauses. He is observing, not performing.*

---

**TAKE: LYDRIX-VO-01**

> "Nexus City runs on The Grid."

*Short pause.*

> "Every light. Every door. Every signal."

*Beat.*

> "Eight million people who never think about the systems keeping them alive."

*Longer pause. Quieter.*

> "I think about them constantly."

*Very quiet. Almost to himself.*

> "It's not a choice."

---

*Record the above as one continuous take.*  
*Then record each sentence individually for editing flexibility.*

## Recording Notes

- Keep all takes in one session if possible for consistency
- Aim for 3 takes of each line minimum

- Mic close, room quiet, warm dry acoustic preferred
