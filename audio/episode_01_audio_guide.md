# Hyperfocus Mode — Episode 1 Audio, VO & Sound Design Guide
## "Overload and Sparks"

**Purpose:** Voice-over, dialogue emphasis, ambient sound, and music cues for Episode 1 assembly.

---

## Global Audio Rules

- **Lydrix POV:** layered city hum, signal noise, muffled voices, electronic whine
- **Hyperfocus Mode:** drop most ambience, replace with clean low pulse + crystalline UI tones
- **Kat / Make It Work:** warm room tone, tools, printer hum, paper, glass clink, mechanical comfort
- **The Static:** low bass drones, data crackle, restrained menace
- **Bolt moments:** soft servo noises, cheerful movement chirps
- **Comedy beats:** let silence and timing do the work before the sound sting

---

## SCENE 1 — COLD OPEN

### Audio Bed
- Deep sub-bass hum
- Clean digital pulse
- Tiny glitch click at anomaly reveal

### Text Cue
- Title card lands with sharp glitch impact

## SCENE 2 — LYDRIX IN THE CROWD

### Lydrix Voice-Over
**LYDRIX (V.O.)**
Nexus City runs on The Grid. Every light. Every door. Every signal.
Eight million people who never think about the systems keeping them alive.
I think about them constantly.
It's not a choice.

### Sound Notes
- Crowd wash, drone buzz, ad screens, transit rumble
- Under the VO, add faint tonal whine from signals only he notices
- When he helps the woman, drop the mix slightly to make the kindness land

### Music
- Minimal atmospheric synth, tense but restrained

## SCENE 6 — LYDRIX ACTIVATES

### Key Dialogue
**LYDRIX**
That's not a glitch. That's a push.

### Hyperfocus Activation Audio
- Crowd noise ducks almost to silence
- Introduce one clean low pulse synced to focus
- Add shimmering circuit tones as scrap rises
- Mechanical clicks as metal locks together

### Music
- Full Hyperfocus Mode theme begins here
- Controlled, uplifting, powerful, not triumphant in a cheesy way

### Sound Effect Beats
- Earphone pull
- Scrap lift
- Rotating metal lock-in
- Relay insertion click
- Systems restore chain reaction

## SCENE 12 — VILLAIN TAG

### Key Dialogue
**STATIC KING**

The cascade today was a test.

### Sound Notes

- Final line over dim screen flicker

### Music
- Villain motif resolves on a dark unfinished note
