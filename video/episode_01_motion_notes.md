# Hyperfocus Mode — Episode 1 Animation Motion Notes
## "Overload and Sparks"

**Purpose:** Camera movement, clip duration, and image-to-video direction for every Episode 1 shot.  
**Use with:** Adobe Firefly image-to-video, DomoAI, Vidu, Runway, Kling, Pika, or manual keyframe animation.

---

## Global Motion Rules

- **Default shot duration:** 3 to 5 seconds
- **Hero shots:** 4 to 6 seconds
- **Comedic beats:** 2 to 3 seconds
- **Villain tension shots:** slower movement, 4 to 5 seconds
- **Lydrix overload POV:** subtle shake + drifting data overlays
- **Hyperfocus Mode:** motion becomes clean, precise, frictionless
- **Kat/workshop scenes:** softer, warmer, slower camera movement
- **Static scenes:** creeping push-ins, cold locked framing
