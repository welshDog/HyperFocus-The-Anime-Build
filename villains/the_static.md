# The Static — Villain Crew

An organised crew of city-destroying system saboteurs who believe the old world should be broken down and rebuilt through chaos. They attack infrastructure, hijack machines, spread panic, and use digital fear like a weapon.

---

## Static King

**Role:** Leader  
**Look:** Tall, still, controlled. Long dark coat with sharp angular lines. High collar or tech visor. One glowing eye detail.  
**Colours:** Black, silver, cold blue  
**Power:** Masterminds every attack. Controls multiple systems simultaneously. Can remotely lock, redirect, or detonate any connected city infrastructure.  
**Vibe:** Calm, cold, terrifyingly intelligent. Treats people like data.

---

## Glitch

**Role:** Infiltrator and chaos agent  
**Look:** Slim, fast, messy energy. Hooded jacket with mismatched tech panels. Asymmetrical hair with neon streaks. Flickering glove effects.  
**Colours:** Neon pink, purple, electric green, white static  
**Power:** Spreads fake alerts, false signals, and decoy chaos. Can fragment and clone digital signals to overwhelm any system.  
**Vibe:** Chaotic, unpredictable, laughs while things fall apart.

---

## ByteWitch

**Role:** Manipulator and code-hacker  
**Look:** Stylish layered cyber fashion. Sleek hair with metallic accents. Eyes that look like screens. Fingers covered in rings, wires, or tech charms.  
**Colours:** Violet, cyan, dark grey, digital glow  
**Power:** Hacks devices, drones, screens, and surveillance. Can rewrite digital behaviour remotely. Uses code like spells.  
**Vibe:** Eerie, elegant, manipulative. Loves distortion and mind games.

---

## Null

**Role:** Silent saboteur  
**Look:** Minimal dark clothing. No flash, no colour. Face covered by plain mask or shadowed hood. Moves like a person-shaped absence.  
**Colours:** Deep black, dull grey, muted red  
**Power:** Creates blackout zones, kills signals, disables systems silently. Specialises in invisible damage before anyone realises it has started.  
**Vibe:** Creepy, emotionless, almost ghost-like.

---

## Overclock

**Role:** Brute force and physical threat  
**Look:** Bigger build, heavy cyber-street gear, exposed mechanical parts, cables, boosted limbs, machine scars, scuffed boots.  
**Colours:** Red, orange, black, burnt metal  
**Power:** Hacked mechanical augmentations give superhuman strength and speed. Turns a digital sabotage mission into a full street-level disaster.  
**Vibe:** Loud, aggressive, destructive. Breaks things for fun.

---

## Crew Dynamic

- Static King = the cold master plan
- Glitch = chaos and misdirection
- ByteWitch = manipulation and control
- Null = silence and sabotage
- Overclock = brute force and destruction

**They are the dark mirror of Lydrix — they corrupt while he rebuilds.**
