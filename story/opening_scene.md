# Hyperfocus Mode — Opening Scene

---

## EXT. CITY STREETS - NIGHT

The city is bright, loud, and crowded. Neon signs buzz overhead. Traffic crawls. Crowds press in on every side.

**LYDRIX** moves through it all with DARK SHADES on and EARPHONES in. He keeps his head down, avoiding faces, avoiding noise, avoiding the world.

A distant SYSTEM ALERT chirps from somewhere in the city.

Then another.

A building screen flickers. A traffic light goes red, then green, then dead.

People start looking around.

A massive DISRUPTION rolls through the network. Doors lock. Phones glitch. Drones spin out. A bus jerks to a stop in the middle of the road.

**CROWD MEMBER**  
What the hell is happening?

Another alarm sounds. Then another. The whole city feels like it's being pulled apart from the inside.

Lydrix stops.

He hears it. Not with his ears — with his mind. The pattern. The break. The attack.

He looks up.

For a second, everything tightens inside him. Then he pulls deeper into himself.

**HYPERFOCUS MODE activates.**

The city sound drops away. The crowd vanishes from his attention. All he sees now are systems, signals, and broken links glowing in the dark.

He reaches out and touches a pile of twisted scrap near the street.

Metal shifts.

Panels lift.

Pieces lock together.

A broken machine becomes a stabiliser. A shattered power unit becomes a shield. A scatter of junk becomes a fast-moving repair construct.

Lydrix rebuilds the collapsing street network in seconds.

Lights return.  
Traffic systems recover.  
The panic begins to settle.

Then silence.

A nearby person stares at him.

**PERSON**  
Where are you going now?

Lydrix lowers his hand. He looks exhausted already.

**LYDRIX**  
No idea. I'm looking for a place.

---

## EXT. KAT'S SHOP - LATER

A small workshop shop front. Hand-painted signs. Warm light in the windows. Tools, art, and maker energy everywhere.

**KAT** stands in the doorway, sleeves rolled up, paint on her hands. She looks tough, practical, and completely unbothered by the chaos outside.

**KAT**  
You can use the storage space upstairs.

**LYDRIX**  
I'm not moving in.

**KAT**  
Sure, mate.

She turns and walks inside like it's already decided.

Lydrix looks at the shop. Then at the city. Then back at the shop.

For the first time, he doesn't keep walking.

**FADE OUT.**
