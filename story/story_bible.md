# Hyperfocus Mode — Story Bible

---

## Logline

A neurodivergent technopath who can turn any material into anything must survive a city under attack from evil hackers while learning how to control his own power without losing himself.

---

## Core Theme

**Being different is not the problem. The world not understanding you is.**

---

## World

A loud, crowded cyberpunk-edged city where technology runs everything. Systems control transport, power, communication, and daily life. The city hums with digital noise — which is great for most people, and overwhelming for Lydrix.

Kat's workshop sits in a quieter pocket of the city. It runs on cash and paperwork with no digital systems, making it the one place hackers cannot touch.

---

## Story Setup

- Evil hackers called The Static are trying to destroy the city.
- Lydrix stops a massive disaster using Hyperfocus Mode.
- Afterward, he has nowhere to go.
- Kat offers storage space above her shop.
- That becomes the base of the story.

---

## Season Arc

1. Lydrix tries to stay hidden.
2. Kat drags him into real connection.
3. The city starts showing signs of tech corruption.
4. Lydrix discovers his power is tied to something bigger.
5. Kat's workshop becomes a safe base and story hub.
6. The Static starts hunting technopaths and gifted creators.
7. Lydrix has to decide whether to keep hiding or step up.
8. He learns Hyperfocus Mode has a cost and control matters as much as power.

---

## Character Arcs

**Lydrix:** Stop hiding. Trust someone. Learn balance. Understand that his mind is not broken.

**Kat:** Learn patience. Understand that not everyone thinks like her. Realise she doesn't need to carry everything alone.

---

- Cyberpunk city energy
