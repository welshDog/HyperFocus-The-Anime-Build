# Kat — Character Sheet

**Full Name:** Katrina "Kat"  
**Role:** Lydrix's closest mate, truth-teller, builder, emotional anchor

---

## Look

- Practical work clothes
- Paint marks, dust, or workshop wear
- Hands that always look busy
- Hair tied back or messy from work
- Strong posture, confident stance
- Often carrying tools, sketches, or a project bag

---

## Personality

- Bold
- Honest and straight-talking
- Caring but not soft
- Quick to call out nonsense
- Always helps before being asked
- Never backs down, even when the truth is not nice

---

## Core Traits

- Fearless in conversation
- Protective of struggling people
- Always trying new techniques
- Hands-on and naturally creative
- Better in chaos than stillness
- Does not understand code or AI
- Works so much she falls asleep if she sits down

---

## Craft Skills

- Painting
- 3D printing
- Glass blowing
- Sculpting
- Repair work
- Prototype building
- Always experimenting with something new

---

## Her Shop

- Part workshop, part art space, part repair hub, part community spot
- Paint everywhere, half-finished prints, glass pieces, tools on walls
- Sketches pinned up, new experiments on every surface
- Storage space upstairs — where Lydrix ends up living
- Cash and paperwork only — no digital trail, untouchable by hackers

---

## Strengths

- Very resourceful
- Good with her hands
- Strong emotional presence
- Great under pressure
- Sees practical solutions fast

---

## Weaknesses

- Too blunt sometimes
- Overcommits to helping people
- Doesn't always slow down
- Can push too hard when others need space
- Falls asleep easily when she stops moving

---

## Relationship with Lydrix

- He goes inward, she goes outward
- He listens to systems, she speaks to people
- He needs silence, she brings motion
- He hides his pain, she names it
- He builds through code, she builds through craft
- She is the one person who gets him

---

## Signature Dialogue Vibe

- "Don't lie to me."
- "I know you're spiralling. Talk."
- "You don't get to vanish just because it's hard."
- "I'm helping. Whether you like it or not."
- "Yeah, that's broken. Give it here."

---

## Theme

Strength can be loud, kind, and creative all at once.
