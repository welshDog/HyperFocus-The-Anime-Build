# Lydrix — Main Character Sheet

**Codename:** Hyperfocus Mode  
**Type:** Neurodivergent Technopath  
**Role:** Main character, lone genius, reluctant creator

---

## Look

- Dark shades, always on
- Earphones in, usually
- Streetwear with a tech edge
- Slightly messy, practical, not flashy
- Glowing circuit effects only when Hyperfocus Mode activates

---

## Personality

- Quiet, sharp, and observant
- Doesn't waste words
- Hates fake energy and big crowds
- Needs space to think
- Dry, unexpected humour
- Feels safest when things are structured

---

## Core Traits

- Hyper-aware of patterns
- Picks up machine logic fast
- Wears shades because eye contact and faces are overwhelming
- Wears earphones to block city noise
- Craves solitude but needs one real mate
- Becomes unstoppable when locked in

---

## Powers

- Sense technology and hidden systems
- Dismantle and rebuild machines
- Turn scrap into tools, weapons, drones, shields, devices
- Hijack nearby tech by touch
- Convert any material into something functional
- Enter Hyperfocus Mode for massive ability boost

**Key rule:** He doesn't create from nothing — he reorganises reality.

---

## Hyperfocus Mode

When activated:
- Sound drops away
- Patterns become visible
- Movements get faster
- He can build insane things from almost anything

Aftermath:
- Exhaustion
- Overstimulation
- Emotional wipeout
- Memory drift in extreme cases

---

## Weaknesses

- Crowds drain him fast
- Noise breaks concentration
- Eye contact and social pressure disrupt focus
- Overusing Hyperfocus causes burnout
- Strong emotions can destabilise precision
- Works best alone or with one trusted person

---

## Theme

Being different is not the flaw. The world not understanding him is.
